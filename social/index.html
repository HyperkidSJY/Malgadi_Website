Social entrepreneurship

"MALGADI SOCIAL INNOVATION” has the vision to solve problems of society And Social entrepreneurship is part of it.

Social entrepreneurs drive social innovation and transformation in various fields including education, health, environment and enterprise development. They pursue poverty alleviation goals with entrepreneurial zeal, business methods and the courage to innovate and overcome traditional practices.

A social entrepreneur, similar to a business entrepreneur, builds strong and sustainable organizations, which are either set up as not-for-profits or companies.Let’s be part of it and learn something innovative and apply in society.
